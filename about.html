About SRJ Tools

Who we are, what we built, and why it matters

What is SRJ Tools?

SRJ Tools is a free, browser-based PDF toolkit that lets you convert, merge, split, compress, protect, and edit PDF files — all without installing anything or creating an account. It was built by SRJahir Tech with one simple goal: give people access to reliable document tools without the usual hassle of paywalls, ads-filled interfaces, or forced signups.

Whether you're a student converting an assignment to PDF, a freelancer merging invoices, or someone who just needs to compress a file before emailing it — SRJ Tools is designed to get the job done quickly and cleanly.

Who Built This?

SRJ Tools is a product of SRJahir Tech, a small independent tech initiative focused on building useful web tools. The project started because we got tired of online PDF converters that either slapped watermarks on your files, limited free conversions, or made you sign up just to merge two PDFs.

We figured — if we can build it better, we should. So we did. Every tool on this platform is completely free and runs directly in your browser or through our secure cloud backend.

How Does It Work?

Most tools on SRJ Tools work in one of two ways:

Browser-based processing: For lighter tasks like rotating pages, previewing PDFs, or reordering pages — everything happens right inside your browser. Your files never leave your device.

Secure cloud processing: For heavier tasks like Word-to-PDF conversion, OCR, or merging large files — your document is sent to our backend API, processed instantly, and returned to you. Files are automatically deleted after processing. We don't store, read, or share your documents — ever.

What Tools Are Available?

SRJ Tools currently offers 18+ free PDF tools across six categories:

Organize: Merge PDF, Split PDF, Remove Pages, Organize PDF

Optimize: Compress PDF, Repair PDF, OCR PDF

Convert To PDF: Word to PDF, Excel to PDF, HTML to PDF

Convert From PDF: PDF to Word, PDF to Excel, PDF to JPG

Edit: Rotate PDF, Add Watermark, Sign PDF

Security: Protect PDF, Unlock PDF

Why Trust SRJ Tools?

There's no catch. No hidden fees, no premium plan upsell, no "you've used your 3 free conversions" nonsense. We built these tools because we needed them ourselves — and we believe good tools should be accessible to everyone.
