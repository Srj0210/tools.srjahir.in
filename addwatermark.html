About SRJ Tools — Add Watermark

Add text or image watermarks to your PDF documents for branding, draft marking, or copyright protection. The watermark appears on every page of your document.

How to Add a Watermark

Step 1: Upload your PDF file.

Step 2: Choose your watermark type — text (like "DRAFT", "CONFIDENTIAL", or your company name) or upload an image (like a logo).

Step 3: Adjust position, size, and opacity as needed.

Step 4: Click "Add Watermark" and download the result.

When to Use Watermarks

Marking draft documents before they're finalized. Adding your company logo to reports. Stamping "CONFIDENTIAL" on sensitive files. Protecting images or creative work shared in PDF format. Watermarks make it clear who owns the content and what its status is.

Common Questions

Q: Can the watermark be removed later?
Once applied, the watermark is embedded in the PDF. Keep your original file if you need an unwatermarked version.

Q: Does it work on all pages?
Yes, the watermark is applied across every page of the document.

You might also want to password-protect the file or compress it after adding a watermark.
