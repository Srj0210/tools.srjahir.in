What's actually free — and what's worth your time

There are hundreds of "free" PDF tools online. The problem is that most of them aren't really free. They'll let you convert one file, then hit you with a paywall. Or they'll add a watermark to the output. Or they'll force you to create an account just to merge two PDFs.

What to Look for in a Free PDF Tool

Before trying any tool, ask yourself these questions: Does it require a signup? Does it add watermarks? Is there a file size limit? How many operations can you do per day? Does it actually delete your files after processing? If a tool fails on more than one of these, it's not really "free" — it's a trial with extra steps.
