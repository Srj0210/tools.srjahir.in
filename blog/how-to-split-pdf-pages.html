Tips for Best Results

💡 Check your result before deleting originals. Always verify the output looks correct before assuming the conversion is perfect — especially for complex layouts or forms.

Use clean source files. The better the input, the better the output. Scanned documents, especially low-quality scans, may need OCR processing first to get accurate results.

Work in batches when possible. If you have multiple files to process, handle them one at a time. This gives you a chance to review each output before moving on.

Keep backups. Before converting or modifying any important document, keep a copy of the original. SRJ Tools processes a copy — your original file is never modified.
