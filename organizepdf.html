About SRJ Tools — Organize PDF

Rearrange pages in your PDF by dragging and dropping them into a new order. See visual thumbnails of every page and put them exactly where you want them.

How to Organize PDF Pages

Step 1: Upload your PDF. Thumbnail previews will load automatically.

Step 2: Drag and drop pages to rearrange them in your preferred order.

Step 3: Once satisfied with the new page order, click "Save" or "Download."

Step 4: Download your reorganized PDF.

When Is This Useful?

When pages got scanned in the wrong order. When you need to move an appendix to the end. When you're assembling a PDF from multiple sources and the page sequence needs adjusting. Anytime the content is right but the order is wrong.

Common Questions

Q: Does it run in the browser?
Yes, page reordering is handled entirely in your browser. Your file isn't uploaded anywhere unless the final save requires server processing.

Q: Is there a page limit?
No hard limit, but very large documents (500+ pages) may load slower due to thumbnail generation.

Need to remove pages entirely? Use Remove Pages. Want to add more pages from another file? Merge PDFs first, then organize.
