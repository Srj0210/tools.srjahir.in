Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The Short Version

We don't sell your data. We don't store your files. We don't require signups. SRJ Tools is built to respect your privacy from the ground up.

1. Who We Are

SRJ Tools is operated by SRJahir Tech. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at tools.srjahir.in.

2. What Data We Collect

Files you upload: When you use a tool that requires server-side processing (like Word to PDF conversion or PDF merging), your file is temporarily sent to our backend for processing. Once the conversion is done and you download the result, the file is automatically deleted. We do not read, copy, or store your files beyond what's needed to complete the task.

Analytics data: We use Google Analytics to understand how people use the site — things like which tools are most popular, what devices people use, and how they found us. This data is anonymized and never tied to your identity.

No personal information: We don't ask for your name, email, phone number, or any personal details to use our tools. There's no account system, no login, and no registration.

3. How We Use Your Data

The only data we "use" is anonymous analytics to improve the site. We look at things like which tools get the most traffic so we can prioritize improvements. That's it. Your uploaded files are processed and discarded — we have no reason to keep them.

4. Third-Party Services

Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics (GA4) to collect anonymized usage statistics. Google may set cookies in your browser for this purpose. You can opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google AdSense: We may display ads through Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your browsing history. You can manage your ad preferences at Google Ad Settings.

5. Cookies

SRJ Tools itself does not set any cookies. However, third-party services like Google Analytics and Google AdSense may place cookies in your browser to function. You can manage or block cookies through your browser settings at any time.

6. Data Security

All file transfers between your browser and our servers happen over HTTPS (encrypted connections). We don't store files on our servers beyond the few seconds needed to process them. Browser-based tools (like Rotate PDF and Organize PDF) never send your files anywhere — everything runs locally on your device.

7. Children's Privacy

SRJ Tools is a general-purpose tool website and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we'll post the changes on this page with a new "Last updated" date. We don't expect major changes since our approach is simple — don't collect what you don't need.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, reach out to us on our Contact page.
