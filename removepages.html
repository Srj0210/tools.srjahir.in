How to Remove Pages

Step 1: Upload your PDF file.

Step 2: Visual thumbnails of every page will be displayed.

Step 3: Select the pages you want to delete.

Common Uses

Removing blank pages from scanned documents. Deleting cover pages or table of contents pages you don't need. Cleaning up a merged PDF that has duplicate pages. Stripping out irrelevant sections before sharing a document.

Common Questions

Q: What's the difference between removing and splitting?
Remove Pages deletes the pages you select and keeps everything else. Split PDF extracts only the pages you select and discards the rest. Use whichever fits your situation.

Q: Can I undo a removal?
The original file isn't modified — you download a new file. Always keep your original as backup.

After removing pages, you might want to reorganize the remaining pages or compress the file.
