About SRJ Tools — PDF Splitter

The SRJ Tools PDF Splitter lets you extract specific pages from any PDF document. Upload your file, see a visual thumbnail preview of every page, select the ones you need, and download a new PDF containing only those pages.

How to Split a PDF

Step 1: Upload your PDF using the file selector above.

Step 2: Thumbnails of all pages will appear automatically. Browse through them to find what you need.

Step 3: Check the boxes next to the pages you want to extract.

Step 4: Click "Split Selected Pages" and download your new PDF.

Features

Visual page preview so you can see exactly what you're selecting. No page limit — works with documents of any length. Fast processing with instant download. Files are automatically deleted after processing for complete privacy.

Common Questions

Q: Does splitting affect quality?
No. Pages are extracted in their original quality — nothing is re-rendered or compressed.

Q: Can I select non-consecutive pages?
Yes. Pick any combination of pages — 1, 5, 12, 20 — and they'll be combined in that order.

Need the opposite? Use Remove Pages to delete specific pages instead. Or try Merge PDF to combine the result with other files.
